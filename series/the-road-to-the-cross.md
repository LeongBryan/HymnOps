## Notes

1. On one hand, there's increasing opposition to Jesus - which demonstrates a hardness of heart - one which refuses to recognise Jesus as Messiah and King.
2. On the another hand, there are the weak, poor and lowly - the children and the blind who recognise Jesus and call Jesus 'Son of David'.
3. The powerful and rich can't enter the kingdom of heaven - but the little children and the blind can.
4. In the middle, there are the disciples - who are often confused and more interested in position than what Jesus is interested in.
